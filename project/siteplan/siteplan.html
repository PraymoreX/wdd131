Website Planning Document

Site Name

HealthBridge

The name “HealthBridge” represents a connection between healthcare and technology, helping users easily access health information and consultation services online.

Optional Domain: healthbridge.com

Site Purpose

This website will provide basic health information, wellness tips, and simple guidance for maintaining a healthy lifestyle. It will also allow users to submit health concerns through a contact form and receive guidance. The platform combines health and technology to improve access to useful health resources.

Scenarios

  • How can I get simple health advice without going to the hospital?
  • What are some daily habits I can follow to improve my health?

Color Scheme

#0a9396 (Teal) – Used for headings and main branding.

#ee9b00 (Orange) – Used for highlights and accents.

#f4f7f8 (Light Gray) – Used as the background color.

Typography

Roboto – Used for both headings and body text to ensure readability and a modern look.
